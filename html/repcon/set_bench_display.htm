Merchandising Standards
 

HOW TO SET A BENCH DISPLAY

 

·        Product should be on separate areas of the same display.  Eight feet per category is recommended. Use the large banners available for the product you are merchandising.

·        Increase or decrease display space as needed dependent on store volume

·        Displays can be back to back or side by side

·        Stripe flats or trays by variety from front to center of the table

·        Stripes should be a minimum of 3 flats per variety

·        Face the flats in a consistent orientation – we recommend broad side forward

·        Labels face forward

·        Affix price card to the front of the flat

·        Retails need to be affixed to each tray

·        Staple or tape tip sheets to benches whenever available

·        Install large program banner to the middle of the display

·        Attach program skirt to the bench

·        Condense product, pull product forward  and keep organized throughout the selling season
